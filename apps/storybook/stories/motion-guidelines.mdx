## Recommended operating ranges

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Micro-interactions">
    Use the fastest and fast duration tokens for hover, focus, and pressed feedback. These should feel immediate and rarely exceed 150ms.
  </Card>
  <Card title="State changes">
    Use the base duration token and standard easing when panels expand, content updates, or tabs switch. This is the default motion layer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Entrances and exits">
    Reserve slower durations for overlays, staged reveals, or patterns where continuity matters. Longer motion needs stronger justification.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
